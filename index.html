About Us

Beaverdale Little League provides quality baseball and softball programs for youth in the Des Moines area. We focus on teaching the fundamentals of the game while instilling values of sportsmanship, teamwork, and dedication. Our league is part of Little League International and follows their rules and regulations.

Leagues & Divisions

Beaverdale Little League offers programs for various age groups:

  • Tee Ball - Ages 4-6: Introduction to baseball fundamentals
  • Rookie - Ages 5-7: Coach pitch and skill development
  • Minors - Ages 7-11: Player pitch baseball
  • Majors - Ages 10-12: Competitive baseball
  • Softball - Various age divisions available

Ages are determined by Little League's age calculator. Visit Little League Age Calculator to determine your child's league age.
